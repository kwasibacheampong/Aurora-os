# Aurora OS

### The Operating System for Marketing in the Age of AI Customers

Aurora OS is a conceptual platform designed to help organisations operate in a world where **AI increasingly mediates discovery and purchasing decisions.**

Two structural shifts are reshaping digital marketing:

• Conversational AI is replacing traditional search

• AI agents are increasingly acting as purchasing decision-makers

Aurora OS provides the infrastructure needed to optimise for both **human customers and machine customers.**

# System Vision

Aurora OS connects marketing influence to commercial outcomes through a closed-loop intelligence system.

AI Discovery
↓
Influence Intelligence
↓
Machine Decision Simulation
↓
Demand Incrementality
↓
Customer Lifetime Value

The platform allows organisations to measure how **AI-driven visibility translates into revenue outcomes.**

# Core Platform Modules

Aurora OS is structured around five core intelligence layers.

## 1. AI Discovery & Influence Intelligence

Tracks brand presence across conversational AI systems.

Capabilities:

• Prompt monitoring

• AI response extraction

• Citation parsing

• Brand mention detection

• Competitor benchmarking

Primary output: Influence Share Index (ISI)

## 2. Generative Engine Optimization (GEO)

Optimises content for AI discovery systems.

Capabilities:

• Citation opportunity detection

• AI readability scoring

• Structured data validation

• Content gap detection

## 3. Machine Customer Simulation

Models how AI agents choose products.

Simulation features:

• Position bias modelling

• Sponsored placement penalties

• Price vs quality trade-offs

• Model-specific decision logic

Primary output: Machine Selection Probability

## 4. Demand & Incrementality Engine

Connects marketing influence to measurable business outcomes.

Capabilities:

• Marketing mix modelling

• Geo lift testing

• Conversational influence attribution

Primary metrics:

• Incremental Demand Yield

• Intent Density Score

## 5. Lifecycle & LTV Intelligence

Predicts long-term customer value.

Capabilities:

• Churn prediction

• Upgrade probability

• Service attachment modelling

• Customer lifetime value forecasting
